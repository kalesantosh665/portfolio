Project Highlights

Some of our most notable aerospace implementations

Agricultural Monitoring Drone System

Completed: June 2022 UAV Design, Precision Agriculture

Developed a custom multi-rotor UAV system equipped with multispectral cameras for crop health monitoring. The solution enabled farmers to reduce water usage by 30% while increasing yields through targeted intervention.

  • 4-hour flight endurance
  • Automated flight planning
  • Real-time data analysis
  • 5km operational range
  • Weather-resistant design
View Case Study

Hybrid VTOL UAV Platform

Completed: March 2023 VTOL, Hybrid Propulsion

Innovative vertical takeoff and landing UAV combining electric and combustion propulsion for extended endurance missions. The platform has been adopted by three research institutions for environmental monitoring.

  • 6-hour flight endurance
  • 15kg payload capacity
  • 50% fuel savings
  • All-weather capability
  • Autonomous operation
View Case Study
