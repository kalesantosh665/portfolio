Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What types of UAVs do you design?

We design a wide range of UAVs including fixed-wing, multi-rotor, hybrid VTOL, and specialized platforms for various applications. Our designs can be customized for payload capacity, flight duration, and operational requirements.

How long does a typical UAV development project take?

Development timelines vary based on complexity, but most projects follow this schedule: Concept phase (2-4 weeks), Design phase (4-8 weeks), Prototyping (4-6 weeks), Testing and refinement (4-8 weeks). We can accelerate timelines for urgent projects.

Do you provide training for operating the UAVs you develop?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training programs tailored to each UAV system we deliver. This includes basic flight operations, maintenance procedures, data collection protocols, and emergency procedures. Training can be conducted at our facility or on-site.

What industries do you serve with your UAV solutions?

Our UAV solutions serve agriculture, energy, construction, mining, defense, environmental monitoring, and logistics sectors. We've also developed specialized solutions for academic research and emergency response applications.

Can you help with regulatory compliance and certification?

Absolutely. Our team includes regulatory experts who can guide you through DGCA and other aviation authority requirements. We assist with documentation, testing protocols, and certification processes to ensure full compliance.
