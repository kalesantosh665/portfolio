About Wings of Aero

Innovating the future of aerospace technology through research, development, and education

Leading Aerospace Innovators

Founded in 2015, Wings of Aero has grown from a passionate startup to a recognized leader in aerospace technology. Our journey began with a small team of engineers and aviation enthusiasts who shared a common vision: to make cutting-edge aerospace technology accessible to everyone.

Today, we operate state-of-the-art research facilities in Bangalore and Hyderabad, collaborating with leading academic institutions and industry partners worldwide. Our interdisciplinary approach combines aerospace engineering with artificial intelligence, materials science, and sustainable energy solutions.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to aerospace innovators

2015

Founded with a Vision

Established by a team of aerospace enthusiasts with a mission to make advanced aerospace technology accessible to students and professionals alike. Our first office was a small garage in Bangalore with just three team members.

2017

First Commercial UAV Project

Successfully completed our first commercial UAV design for agricultural monitoring, marking our entry into the professional aerospace sector. This project helped farmers increase crop yields by 18%.

2019

Research Partnership Established

Formed strategic partnerships with leading technical universities to advance aerospace research and development. Launched our first joint research program on sustainable aviation fuels.

2020

Pandemic Response

Developed drone delivery systems for medical supplies during the COVID-19 pandemic, serving remote communities across three states and delivering over 5,000 critical packages.

2022

National Recognition

Awarded "Most Innovative Aerospace Startup" by the Indian Aerospace Association for our work in drone technology education and our hybrid propulsion system innovations.

2023

Global Expansion

Opened our first international office in Singapore and partnered with three European aerospace firms to develop next-generation urban air mobility solutions.

Our Technology

Pioneering solutions that redefine aerospace possibilities

Advanced Propulsion Systems

Our hybrid-electric propulsion technology delivers 40% greater efficiency than conventional systems while reducing emissions by 60%. The modular design allows for easy maintenance and upgrades.

AI Flight Control

Proprietary machine learning algorithms enable autonomous decision-making in complex flight environments with 99.9% reliability. Our systems continuously learn and adapt to new scenarios.

Cybersecurity

Military-grade encryption and blockchain-based communication protocols ensure complete data security for all our systems. Regular penetration testing maintains our robust security standards.

Energy Storage

Our proprietary battery technology offers 3x the energy density of conventional lithium-ion batteries, with faster charging times and improved thermal stability for aerospace applications.

Communication Systems

Next-gen mesh networking enables seamless communication between aircraft and ground stations, even in remote areas with limited infrastructure.

Sustainable Materials

We've developed lightweight composite materials using recycled carbon fiber and plant-based resins, reducing the environmental impact of aircraft manufacturing by up to 45%.

Our Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure for aerospace innovation

Bangalore R&D Center

Our 25,000 sq ft research hub features advanced wind tunnels, materials testing labs, and one of India's largest drone development facilities. The center operates 24/7 with a team of 50 researchers.

Hyderabad Manufacturing Plant

A fully automated production facility capable of manufacturing up to 200 UAVs per month. The plant features robotic assembly lines and rigorous quality control systems meeting ISO 9001 and AS9100 standards.

Aero Academy

Our education center features flight simulators, VR training systems, and hands-on workshops. Over 2,000 students have graduated from our programs, with 85% securing positions in the aerospace industry.

Chennai Test Range

A 500-acre secured facility for flight testing and certification. The range includes multiple landing zones, obstacle courses, and all-weather testing capabilities approved by DGCA.
