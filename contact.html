Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and processes

We design a wide range of UAVs including multi-rotor drones for aerial photography and inspection, fixed-wing drones for long-range operations, and hybrid VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing) drones. Our designs can be customized for various payloads and mission requirements.

We offer workshops for individuals, educational institutions, and corporate groups. You can check our upcoming workshop schedule on our website or contact us to organize a custom workshop for your group. No prior experience is required for our beginner-level workshops.

Our UAV solutions serve multiple industries including agriculture (crop monitoring), energy (solar panel and wind turbine inspection), construction (site surveying), environmental (wildlife monitoring), and public safety (search and rescue operations).

Yes, we offer internship programs for engineering students interested in aerospace technology. Interns work on real projects under the guidance of our experienced team. Check our Careers page for current openings or send us your resume with a cover letter.

The timeline varies based on complexity, but a typical custom UAV project takes 3-6 months from initial consultation to final delivery. This includes requirements analysis, design, prototyping, testing, and certification. Simpler projects can be completed in 4-8 weeks.

Yes, we provide comprehensive maintenance and repair services for all types of UAVs, including those not originally designed by us. Our service packages include regular maintenance, software updates, hardware repairs, and performance optimization.

Our training programs are DGCA-compliant and provide certification recognized by the Indian aviation authorities. We offer both basic and advanced certifications for commercial drone operations, including specialized certifications for specific industries.
